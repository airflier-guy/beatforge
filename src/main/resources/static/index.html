What is BeatForge?

BeatForge is a free, browser-based beat sequencer with both Western and Indian instruments. Make beats with Tabla, Dhol, Sitar, Dholak alongside Kick, Snare and more.

Download your beats as WAV files, save multiple patterns, and share them with friends via a link — all for free, no signup needed.
